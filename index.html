Friendly Questions We Hear Every Day

Straight answers — no grooming jargon, just what you actually need to know.

Most appointments take 1.5 to 3 hours, depending on your dog's size, coat type, and the service selected. Small dogs with short coats are usually done in about 90 minutes, while large dogs with long or double coats can take closer to 3 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

It depends on the breed and coat type. As a general rule: short-coat dogs every 8–12 weeks, medium-coat dogs every 6–8 weeks, and long or double-coat dogs every 4–6 weeks. Regular grooming prevents painful matting and keeps your pup healthy and comfortable.

Absolutely — many dogs feel nervous the first time. Our groomers are trained to work gently with anxious pets. We take breaks when needed, use calm voices and slow movements, and never rush your dog. Please let us know in advance if your pet has anxiety so we can prepare and give extra care.

Our Full Grooming Package includes: bath with premium shampoo & conditioner, blow-dry, brush-out, haircut to your preferred style, nail trim, ear cleaning, and teeth brushing. Basically everything your dog needs to feel — and smell — amazing from nose to tail.

We'll always do our best to de-mat gently, but in severe cases the most humane option is a shave-down to relieve discomfort. We'll discuss this with you before doing anything. To avoid matting, we recommend regular brushing at home and sticking to a consistent grooming schedule.

Yes! We groom cats and some small animals as well. Cat grooming requires a different approach and handling technique, so please call us first to discuss your cat's temperament and needs. We want to make sure every visit is a good experience for your pet.

A few simple things help a lot: bring vaccination records (required for first-time visits), try a short walk beforehand so your dog arrives calm, and let your pet use the bathroom before the appointment. If your dog has any health conditions or sensitivities, mention them when you book.

We strongly recommend booking at least 2–3 days ahead, especially on weekends — spots fill up fast! Same-day appointments are occasionally available, so it's always worth calling. The easiest way to get started is to fill out our quick quiz above and we'll call you back within the hour.
