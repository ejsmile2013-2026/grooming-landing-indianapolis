Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or complete our grooming quiz, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact details — your name, phone number, and email address (provided voluntarily through our quiz or contact form)
  • Pet information — pet name, breed, size, and coat type (to provide accurate service estimates)
  • Usage data — pages visited and time spent on the site (collected anonymously, without cookies)

We do not collect payment information on this website. All payments are processed in person at our salon.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Contact you to schedule or confirm grooming appointments
  • Provide you with an accurate service estimate based on your pet's needs
  • Respond to questions you send through our contact form
  • Send occasional service reminders (only if you opt in)

We will never use your information for unrelated marketing, and we will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools that follow you across the internet. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar tracking scripts.

We may use session storage (a temporary browser feature) to remember your quiz progress within a single visit. This data is automatically deleted when you close your browser.

4. Data Storage & Security

Information submitted through our quiz or contact form is sent securely to our appointment management system (Make.com) and may be stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to our staff. We use HTTPS encryption on all pages of this website.

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to provide our services and will delete it upon your request.

5. Indiana Privacy Rights

If you are an Indiana resident, under the Indiana Consumer Data Protection Act you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites such as Yelp, Google Maps, and Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
